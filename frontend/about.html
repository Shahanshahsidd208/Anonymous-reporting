About Us

The Hubballi-Dharwad Police are dedicated to ensuring the safety and security of the city's residents. They maintain a vigilant presence across all neighborhoods and are equipped to respond swiftly to any incidents. The force is committed to community policing, fostering a sense of trust and collaboration between law enforcement and the public.

Our History

Hubballi Dharwad Police Commissionerate was carved out of Dharwad District and established in the year 1989, vide Government Order No: HD-91/POP/1989, Dated: 17-10-1989. The commissionerate area covers 202.3 Sq.Kms. The unit have 4 Sub-Divisions, 15 law and order Police Stations, 4 Traffic Police Stations and 2 Special Police Stations.

About Our Website

Anonymous Reporter is an innovative platform designed to facilitate secure and anonymous crime reporting. Our mission is to empower individuals to report incidents without fear of retaliation or breach of privacy. By using our service, users can contribute to the safety of their community while remaining anonymous.

Our system is simple and user-friendly: users can scan QR codes placed strategically throughout Hubballi-Dharwad to access our web interface. Once on the platform, they can report incidents by providing their phone number and enabling location access. This ensures that precise location details are captured, which are crucial for effective incident management and response.

Upon submission, the information is securely transmitted to our backend, where it is processed and sent to the appropriate authorities via SMS using the Twilio API. This method ensures that the reports are quickly and efficiently relayed to law enforcement, enabling prompt action.
